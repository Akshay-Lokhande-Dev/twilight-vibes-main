What This Site Is

TwilightVibes is an independently run fan site built for people who love the Twilight Saga and want a single, organised place to find information about it — where it was filmed, what the real towns of Forks and La Push are like, who is in the cast, and how the books and films connect.

This site started because, like a lot of fans, we kept noticing that good information about Twilight's real-world locations was scattered across forums, outdated blog posts, and old news articles. TwilightVibes pulls that information together in one place: filming location details, location-comparison guides (book setting vs. actual film locations), cast updates, and the saga's lore, organised so it's easy to find.

How We Research

Content on this site is compiled and written using publicly available sources — official film production information, news coverage, interviews, tourism board listings, and the experiences shared by the wider Twilight fan community. Where a travel detail can change (opening hours, festival dates, accommodation), we link out to the official source so you can confirm current information directly before you travel.

We are not claiming to have personally visited every location described on this site. Where a guide discusses what a place is actually like to visit, that's drawn from publicly available travel reporting and fan accounts rather than presented as first-hand experience.

Why TwilightVibes

The recent Twilight Renaissance — TikTok rediscovering the saga, a new generation of readers, and renewed interest in a possible Midnight Sun adaptation — brought a wave of fans asking the same questions: Is Forks worth visiting? Where exactly were the scenes filmed? What's the difference between the book's Washington setting and the film's Oregon locations? TwilightVibes exists to answer those questions clearly, in one place.

Corrections & Updates

If you spot something inaccurate, outdated, or have first-hand travel experience you'd like to share for a future update, we'd genuinely like to hear from it — see contact details below. Fan-submitted corrections and on-the-ground updates help keep this guide accurate.

This site uses affiliate links in some recommendations (books, merchandise, accommodation), which are always disclosed and never influence which locations or businesses are covered.

TwilightVibes has no official affiliation with Lionsgate, Summit Entertainment, Stephenie Meyer, or the Quileute Nation. This is an independent fan site created out of love for the story and the landscape that inspired it.
