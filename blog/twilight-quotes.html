Twilight Quotes

The lines that stayed with us — from all five films and the books.

TwilightVibes › Universe Guide › Quotes

Some lines from the Twilight Saga became part of a generation's cultural memory — whispered at sleepovers, written in notebooks, still surfacing on social media fifteen years after the films were made. This is the definitive collection of the most iconic, most beloved, and most quoted lines from all five films and Stephenie Meyer's original books.

Edward Cullen — The Best Lines

💙 Romantic
And so the lion fell in love with the lamb.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008). One of the most quoted lines in the entire saga.
🌙 Obsessive
I decided as long as I'm going to hell, I might as well do it thoroughly.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008). Said before kissing Bella for the first time.
🌿 Danger
Everything about you invites me in — your voice, your scent, the way you move. I should be avoiding you. And yet here I am.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (novel), adapted across the film.
❤️ Devotion
You are my life now.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008). Simple. Absolute. The most Edward sentence possible.
🌑 New Moon
I'm not strong enough to stay away from you, Bella.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008).
💔 Heartbreak
You just have to understand that no matter how much I love you, I'm not what you need.
— Edward Cullen, New Moon (2009). The forest scene that broke everyone.

Bella Swan — Her Best Lines

🐑 Iconic
What a stupid lamb.
— Bella Swan, Twilight (2008). Her response to Edward's lion-and-lamb speech. Became one of the most quoted lines in the saga.
🌧️ Forks
Forks was literally my own personal hell on Earth.
— Bella Swan, Twilight (novel). Her first impression — before the vampires.
💜 Eclipse
I'm not good enough to be your friend anymore, let alone anything else. I love you. It's a problem.
— Bella Swan, Eclipse (2010), to Jacob.
🔥 Breaking Dawn
I was born to be a vampire.
— Bella Swan-Cullen, Breaking Dawn Part 2 (2012). Her transformation complete.

Jacob Black — His Best Lines

🐺 Territory
Does it bother you, me being here? We're not supposed to be friends, you and I.
— Jacob Black, Twilight (2008), at First Beach, La Push.
🔥 Warmth
I'm exactly right for you, Bella. It would have been effortless for us — comfortable, easy as breathing.
— Jacob Black, Eclipse (novel). The line that made Team Jacob a real debate.
💛 New Moon
You're my best friend. I don't want to lose that.
— Jacob Black, New Moon (2009). Before everything got complicated.

The Unforgettable Supporting Lines

👁️ Volturi
What a happy surprise. Bella is alive after all.
— Aro, New Moon (2009). Michael Sheen making every word land like a velvet threat.
🌟 Alice
You're going to be my new sister. I've always wanted a sister.
— Alice Cullen, Twilight (2008). The warmth that made Alice everyone's favourite Cullen.
🌲 Carlisle
We all have the ability to be good. We all have the ability to choose.
— Dr. Carlisle Cullen, Twilight (novel). The philosophical core of the Cullen way of life.
😂 Charlie
I'm not going to arrest him or anything. I just want to talk to the guy.
— Charlie Swan, Twilight (2008). Billy Burke delivering classic dad energy.
⚾ Emmett
America's favorite pastime.
— Emmett Cullen, Twilight (2008). Before the most cinematic vampire baseball scene ever filmed.

The Most Quoted Lines of the Entire Saga

Based on cultural impact, social media presence, and fan community consensus, these are the lines that have endured the longest across the Twilight fandom:

🏆 #1 Most Quoted
And so the lion fell in love with the lamb... What a stupid lamb. What a sick, masochistic lion.
— Edward and Bella, Twilight (2008). The exchange that defined the saga's central dynamic.
🏆 #2
I decided as long as I'm going to hell, I might as well do it thoroughly.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008).
🏆 #3
You are my life now.
— Edward Cullen, Twilight (2008). Three words. Maximum impact.
