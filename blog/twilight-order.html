Twilight Books in Reading Order

The Twilight book series has a clear reading order — four main novels plus additional companion books. Here is the definitive sequence:

1
Twilight (2005)Bella arrives in Forks, meets Edward, discovers he is a vampire, and chooses him. James hunts her. The beginning of everything.
2
New Moon (2006)Edward leaves. Bella's grief, her friendship with Jacob, the wolf pack, and the Volturi. The darkest and most psychologically honest book.
3
Eclipse (2007)The love triangle reaches its peak. Victoria's newborn army. Bella's choice between Edward and Jacob. The engagement.
+
The Short Second Life of Bree Tanner (2010)An Eclipse novella. Tells the story of the newborn army from Bree's perspective. Best read right after finishing Eclipse.
4
Breaking Dawn (2008)The wedding, the honeymoon, Renesmee's birth, Bella's transformation, the Volturi confrontation. The conclusion.
+
Midnight Sun (2020)Twilight retold from Edward's perspective. Reveals his thoughts during every scene of the first book. Read after Twilight or as a standalone revisit.
+
Life and Death (2015)A gender-swapped retelling of Twilight — Beau Swan and Edythe Cullen. A standalone companion, not part of the main series. Optional but interesting.
Best reading order for newcomers: Read Twilight → New Moon → Eclipse → Bree Tanner → Breaking Dawn. Then Midnight Sun if you want Edward's perspective on book one.

Twilight Movies in Watching Order

The five Twilight films follow the books directly, with Breaking Dawn split into two parts. Watch in release order — this is both the chronological and the intended order.

1
Twilight (2008)Directed by Catherine Hardwicke. Filmed primarily in Oregon. Sets the visual tone for the entire saga. Kristen Stewart, Robert Pattinson, Taylor Lautner.
2
New Moon (2009)Directed by Chris Weitz. Production moves to Vancouver. Volturi in Montepulciano, Italy. The wolf pack's first full appearance.
3
Eclipse (2010)Directed by David Slade. The newborn army battle. The tent scene. The clearest visual realisation of the Jacob-Bella-Edward triangle.
4
Breaking Dawn Part 1 (2011)Directed by Bill Condon. The wedding and honeymoon (filmed in Brazil). Renesmee's birth. Bella's near-death and transformation begins.
5
Breaking Dawn Part 2 (2012)Directed by Bill Condon. Bella as a newborn vampire. Gathering witnesses. The field confrontation with the Volturi. The epic (and shocking) movie ending.
Where to watch: As of 2026, the Twilight films frequently rotate across streaming platforms like Netflix, Peacock, and Amazon Prime Video. Check JustWatch for current streaming options in your country.

Quick Reference

Total reading time (main 4 books): approximately 50–60 hours. Total watch time (all 5 films): approximately 11 hours. If you only have one weekend: read Twilight, watch Twilight, then decide if you continue.
