The Twilight Cast

Every actor, every character, across all five films.

TwilightVibes › Universe Guide › The Cast

The Twilight Saga ran from 2008 to 2012 and assembled one of the most memorable ensemble casts of the 2000s. Many of the actors — then largely unknown — went on to major careers. Here is the complete guide to every significant cast member across all five films, what they brought to their roles, and where they are now.

The Core Three

Kristen Stewart
Bella Swan
The emotional anchor of all five films. Stewart brought intense interiority to a role that was easy to dismiss, making Bella's choices feel genuinely weighted. Since Twilight: Spencer (2021), Personal Shopper, Crimes of the Future, and many more — widely considered one of the best actresses working today.
Robert Pattinson
Edward Cullen
The vampire who had to be beautiful, dangerous, and lovestruck all at once. Pattinson made Edward compelling through sheer intensity of presence. Since Twilight: Good Time, The Lighthouse, Tenet, The Batman (2022) as Bruce Wayne — one of the most critically acclaimed actors of his generation.
Taylor Lautner
Jacob Black
The warm-blooded counterpoint to Edward's icy control. Lautner's physical transformation between films was remarkable and became part of pop culture history. Since Twilight: He largely stepped back from Hollywood, marrying Taylor Dome in 2022, and has returned to occasional projects and social media.

The Cullen Family

Peter Facinelli
Dr. Carlisle Cullen
The compassionate patriarch — 300+ years old and still practicing medicine. Facinelli's calm authority anchored the Cullen family dynamic across all five films.
Elizabeth Reaser
Esme Cullen
The nurturing heart of the Cullen household. Reaser has continued a strong TV career since Twilight, notably in The Haunting of Hill House and The Haunting of Bly Manor.
Ashley Greene
Alice Cullen
The pixie-cut visionary with a gift for future sight and an infectious enthusiasm that made her a fan favourite. Greene has continued acting and is very active in the fan community.
Kellan Lutz
Emmett Cullen
The physically powerful, perpetually cheerful muscle of the Cullen family. The baseball scene's most exuberant participant. Lutz has continued acting in action and thriller projects.
Nikki Reed
Rosalie Hale
The most ambivalent Cullen — stunning, proud, and deeply conflicted about her vampire existence. Reed married Ian Somerhalder (The Vampire Diaries) in 2015 and has become a prominent wellness and sustainability advocate.
Jackson Rathbone
Jasper Hale
The Confederate-era vampire with the ability to manipulate emotions — and the most visibly uncomfortable with his restraint. Rathbone's performance captured Jasper's constant internal war memorably.

The Wolf Pack

Chaske Spencer
Sam Uley
The pack alpha — composed, powerful, and burdened by what the transformation costs. Spencer has gone on to significant TV work including Rutherford Falls.
Alex Meraz
Paul
The most volatile member of the pack — quick-tempered, fast to shift, and fierce in battle. Meraz brought believable unpredictability to Paul's explosive scenes.
Bronson Pelletier
Jared
The loyal, steady member of the pack whose ease with the wolf life contrasted with Jacob's resistance.
Kiowa Gordon
Embry Call
One of Jacob's closest friends before and after the transformation, with a bond to the pack that felt genuinely warm across the ensemble.
Tyson Houseman
Quil Ateara
Part of Jacob's inner circle, with one of the more unusual subplot arcs in the later books that the films handled with appropriate restraint.
BooBoo Stewart
Seth Clearwater
Leah's younger brother — the youngest, most optimistic member of the pack, and Jacob's most earnest ally in Eclipse and Breaking Dawn.

Forks Humans — Bella's School Friends

Billy Burke
Charlie Swan
The quiet, emotionally contained Forks police chief and Bella's father — arguably the film series' most consistent scene-stealer. Burke's dry, understated performance became one of the most beloved aspects of the entire saga. He has continued a very strong acting career since.
Anna Kendrick
Jessica Stanley
Bella's bubbly, slightly competitive best friend at Forks High. Kendrick brought sharp comic timing that made Jessica far more entertaining than the role required — she has since become a major Hollywood star.
Michael Welch
Mike Newton
The good-natured, hopelessly infatuated boy who never stood a chance against Edward. Mike's confused friendliness was a useful human anchor to the more supernatural elements.
Christian Serratos
Angela Weber
Bella's genuinely kind friend — the one human connection that felt warm without being played for laughs. Serratos went on to play Rosita in The Walking Dead for nine seasons.
Justin Chon
Eric Yorkie
The school journalist and persistent admirer, providing reliable comic relief throughout the high school scenes. Chon has since become a respected writer-director of independent films.

The Volturi

Michael Sheen
Aro
The delighted, terrifying ruler of the vampire world — Sheen's Aro is one of the saga's greatest pleasures, performing ancient malevolence with barely contained glee. His laugh in Breaking Dawn Part 2 became one of the most beloved memes of the franchise.
Dakota Fanning
Jane
The Volturi's most feared weapon — able to create the illusion of unbearable pain with a glance. Fanning's cold stillness made Jane genuinely unsettling across all her appearances.
Jamie Campbell Bower
Caius (young Aro — New Moon)
Also played young Caius in the New Moon flashback. Later went on to major fame as Vecna in Stranger Things Season 4.
Cameron Bright
Alec
Jane's twin — the Volturi's other ultimate weapon, able to numb all senses. Bright's quiet menace complemented Fanning's icy cruelty effectively.

The Villains

Cam Gigandet
James
The tracker — the primary antagonist of the first film. Gigandet's dangerous physicality and predatory stillness made James a genuinely threatening villain.
Rachelle Lefevre / Bryce Dallas Howard
Victoria
The fiery, revenge-driven mate of James. Lefevre played Victoria in Twilight and New Moon; Howard replaced her for Eclipse due to a scheduling conflict — a casting change that remained controversial with fans.
Xavier Samuel
Riley Biers
Victoria's unwitting pawn in Eclipse — the young vampire who believed he was loved while being used to build her newborn army. Samuel's performance brought real pathos to a character with limited screen time.
Fun fact: Robert Pattinson had already played Cedric Diggory in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005) before being cast as Edward — making him the first actor to have major roles in both franchises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who plays Edward Cullen in Twilight?

Edward Cullen is played by Robert Pattinson across all five Twilight films (2008–2012). Pattinson has since gone on to become one of the most critically acclaimed actors of his generation, starring in The Lighthouse, Good Time, and The Batman (2022).

Who plays Bella Swan in Twilight?

Bella Swan is played by Kristen Stewart in all five films. Stewart has since become one of Hollywood's most respected actresses, earning an Academy Award nomination for Spencer (2021).

Who plays Jacob Black in Twilight?

Jacob Black is played by Taylor Lautner. Lautner's physical transformation for New Moon — gaining approximately 30 pounds of muscle to keep the role — became one of the most discussed aspects of the franchise's production.

Why was Victoria recast in Eclipse?

Rachelle Lefevre, who played Victoria in Twilight and New Moon, was replaced by Bryce Dallas Howard for Eclipse due to a scheduling conflict with another film (Barney's Version). The recasting was controversial with fans, and Lefevre herself publicly expressed disappointment about the change.

Where is the Twilight cast now in 2026?

Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart are both highly active in prestigious film projects. Taylor Lautner has taken a lower profile. Ashley Greene, Kellan Lutz, Nikki Reed, and Jackson Rathbone remain active in entertainment. Billy Burke and Anna Kendrick have had the strongest sustained TV and film careers among the supporting cast. Christian Serratos had a long run on The Walking Dead. Jamie Campbell Bower became globally famous as Vecna in Stranger Things Season 4 (2022).
