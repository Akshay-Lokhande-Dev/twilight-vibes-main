The Twilight Cast

Every actor, every character, across all five films — and where they are now in 2026.

The Twilight Saga ran from 2008 to 2012 and assembled one of the most memorable ensemble casts of the 2000s. Many of the actors — then largely unknown — went on to major careers. Here is the complete guide to every significant cast member across all five films, what they brought to their roles, and where they are now.

The Core Three

The Cullen Family

Peter Facinelli
Dr. Carlisle Cullen
The compassionate 300-year-old patriarch who somehow manages to be a surgeon without losing control. Facinelli's quiet authority gave the Cullen family its moral centre. Since Twilight he has starred in Nurse Jackie, The Neighborhood, and multiple film projects. He is a regular at Twilight fan conventions and reunited with the Cullen cast at Epic Cons Chicago in 2024.
Elizabeth Reaser
Esme Cullen
The warm, endlessly nurturing heart of the Cullen household who gave up everything human — and found a better family. Reaser's post-Twilight career is arguably the strongest of the Cullen ensemble: The Haunting of Hill House, The Haunting of Bly Manor (both Netflix, massive critical hits), and recurring prestige TV roles. One of the most underrated actors in the franchise.
Ashley Greene
Alice Cullen
The pixie-cut, future-seeing, fashion-obsessed fan favourite who became Bella's closest vampire ally. Alice's visions drove the plot of three films. Greene married rugby player Paul Khoury in 2018 and gave birth to their daughter Kingsley in 2022. She remains very active in the Twilight fan community and was part of the Cullen family reunion at Epic Cons Chicago in 2024.
Kellan Lutz
Emmett Cullen
The physically massive, perpetually cheerful muscle of the Cullen family — the one who always seems to be genuinely enjoying being a vampire. Lutz's baseball scene remains one of the most purely fun moments of the saga. Since Twilight he has starred in FBI: Most Wanted on CBS and multiple action films. He and wife Brittany Gonzales welcomed a daughter in 2019. Reunited with the Cullens at Emerald City Comic Con 2024.
Nikki Reed
Rosalie Hale
The most conflicted Cullen — achingly beautiful, deeply bitter about what she lost, and the one who most wants the human life she was robbed of. Reed's performance gave Rosalie far more depth than the books. Off-screen, Reed married Vampire Diaries star Ian Somerhalder in 2015 — yes, she married another vampire actor. They have a daughter, Bodhi, born 2017. Reed has become a prominent wellness, sustainability, and ethical fashion advocate.
Jackson Rathbone
Jasper Hale
The Confederate-era vampire who can manipulate the emotions of everyone around him — and looks visibly pained by every meal. Rathbone's portrayal of Jasper's constant internal struggle became a beloved source of fan affection and memes. He is an active musician (leading the band 100 Monkeys) and appeared alongside his Cullen co-stars at multiple fan conventions through 2024.

Forks Humans — Bella's School Friends

Billy Burke
Charlie Swan
Bella's quiet, emotionally restrained father and the Forks police chief — arguably the saga's most consistently underrated and beloved character. Burke's dry, deadpan delivery made Charlie the audience's most relatable anchor throughout all five films. Since Twilight he has starred in Revolution (NBC), Zoo (CBS), Removed, and multiple film roles. Consistently cited by fans as the character they'd most want to have a beer with.
Anna Kendrick
Jessica Stanley
Bella's slightly competitive, gossip-prone best friend who delivered some of the saga's sharpest comic moments. Kendrick's timing made Jessica far more entertaining than the role required on paper. Since Twilight: Up in the Air (Oscar nomination), Pitch Perfect franchise, A Simple Favor, Alice Darling. Currently filming Embassy (spy thriller series with J.K. Simmons, London 2026) and comedy Babies with Seth Rogen. Reportedly dating British comedian Alex Edelman since late 2024.
Michael Welch
Mike Newton
The good-natured, hopelessly smitten boy-next-door who never stood a chance against a century-old vampire. Mike's bumbling affection served as the franchise's most reliable source of gentle comedy and humanised the Forks school setting effectively across all five films.
Christian Serratos
Angela Weber
Bella's genuinely kind, non-judgemental school friend — the one human relationship that felt warm without comic exaggeration. Serratos went on to play Rosita Espinosa in The Walking Dead for nine full seasons, one of the longest runs in that show's history. A remarkable post-Twilight career ascent.
Justin Chon
Eric Yorkie
The school journalist and Bella's persistent, enthusiastic admirer — providing consistent comic relief across the franchise. Chon has since become a critically respected independent filmmaker, writing and directing Gook (2017, Sundance), Ms. Purple (2019), and Blue Bayou (2021, Cannes). One of the most impressive directorial careers to emerge from the Twilight cast.

The Volturi

Introduced in New Moon (2009), the Volturi are the ancient, ruling class of the vampire world — based in Volterra, Italy. Michael Sheen's portrayal of Aro became one of the franchise's most celebrated performances and a source of enduring meme culture.

Michael Sheen
Aro
The ancient, theatrically delighted ruler of the vampire world — the Volturi's supreme leader who performs absolute power with barely-contained glee. Sheen's Aro is widely considered one of the saga's greatest performances, bringing Shakespearean camp to a character who could have been merely sinister. His laugh in Breaking Dawn Part 2 became the franchise's most beloved meme. Since Twilight: Good Omens (Amazon), Quiz, The Damned. Widely regarded as one of the finest British actors of his generation.
Dakota Fanning
Jane
The Volturi's most feared weapon — able to create the illusion of unbearable pain with a single glance. Fanning's cold, almost bored stillness made Jane genuinely unsettling in every scene. Since Twilight she has built a formidable arthouse career: The Runaways, Effie Gray, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, The Alienist, and The Watchers (2024). One of the most respected actresses of her generation.
Jamie Campbell Bower
Young Caius / Volturi Guard
Started in Twilight as a minor Volturi figure — and went on to achieve one of the most dramatic career ascents of any franchise cast member. Bower played Vecna / Henry Creel in Stranger Things Season 4 (2022), arguably the show's most celebrated villain performance. His casting reveal became one of Netflix's biggest moments of 2022. A remarkable full-circle for a Twilight actor.
Cameron Bright
Alec
Jane's twin brother and the Volturi's other ultimate weapon — able to numb all senses simultaneously, rendering victims helpless before the kill. Bright's quiet, unnerving menace complemented Fanning's icy cruelty perfectly across New Moon, Eclipse, and Breaking Dawn.

The Villains

Cam Gigandet
James
The tracker — a vampire who hunts purely for sport — and the primary antagonist of the original Twilight (2008). Gigandet's predatory stillness and dangerous physicality made James one of the saga's most effective villains in limited screen time. The ballet studio confrontation remains one of the franchise's tensest sequences.
Rachelle Lefevre / Bryce Dallas Howard
Victoria
The fiery, revenge-obsessed mate of James whose pursuit of Bella drives the threat across three films. Rachelle Lefevre played Victoria in Twilight and New Moon with feral, red-haired intensity. She was controversially replaced by Bryce Dallas Howard for Eclipse due to a scheduling conflict — a recasting that Lefevre publicly called devastating and that fans never fully accepted. Howard's version was capable but the switch broke continuity the audience felt.
Xavier Samuel
Riley Biers
Victoria's unwitting general in Eclipse — the young man who genuinely believed he was loved while being manipulated into building a newborn army destined for slaughter. Samuel brought unexpected pathos to a character with limited screen time, making Riley one of the more tragic figures in the franchise. Since Twilight he has worked steadily in Australian and international cinema.
⚡ Fun fact: Robert Pattinson had already played Cedric Diggory in Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire (2005) before being cast as Edward — making him the first actor to have major roles in both franchises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who plays Edward Cullen in Twilight?

Edward Cullen is played by Robert Pattinson across all five Twilight films (2008–2012). Pattinson has since become one of the most critically acclaimed actors of his generation, starring in The Lighthouse, Good Time, and The Batman (2022) as Bruce Wayne.

Who plays Bella Swan in Twilight?

Bella Swan is played by Kristen Stewart in all five films. Stewart has since become one of Hollywood's most respected actresses, earning an Academy Award nomination for Spencer (2021).

Who plays Jacob Black in Twilight?

Jacob Black is played by Taylor Lautner. Lautner's physical transformation for New Moon — gaining approximately 30 pounds of muscle to keep the role — became one of the most discussed aspects of the franchise's production.

Why was Victoria recast in Eclipse?

Rachelle Lefevre, who played Victoria in Twilight and New Moon, was replaced by Bryce Dallas Howard for Eclipse due to a scheduling conflict with another film (Barney's Version). The recasting was controversial with fans, and Lefevre herself publicly expressed disappointment about the change.

Where is the Twilight cast now in 2026?

Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart are both highly active in prestigious film projects. Taylor Lautner has taken a lower profile. Ashley Greene, Kellan Lutz, Nikki Reed, and Jackson Rathbone remain active in entertainment. Billy Burke and Anna Kendrick have had the strongest sustained TV and film careers among the supporting cast. Christian Serratos had a long run on The Walking Dead. Jamie Campbell Bower became globally famous as Vecna in Stranger Things Season 4 (2022).

How many Twilight films are there?

There are five Twilight films in total: Twilight (2008), The Twilight Saga: New Moon (2009), The Twilight Saga: Eclipse (2010), The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 1 (2011), and The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2 (2012). The series collectively grossed over $3.3 billion worldwide.

Is Kristen Stewart still acting in 2026?

Yes — more than ever. Kristen Stewart made her directorial debut with The Chronology of Water (Cannes 2025, 91% on Rotten Tomatoes) and is set to star as NASA astronaut Sally Ride in Amazon's The Challenger limited series in 2026. She also stars in Flesh of the Gods alongside Elizabeth Olsen.

Is Kristen Stewart married?

Yes. Kristen Stewart married screenwriter Dylan Meyer on April 20, 2025, in an intimate ceremony in Los Angeles. The couple got engaged in 2021. Stewart has spoken about wanting to start a family.

What is Robert Pattinson doing in 2026?

Robert Pattinson is having one of his biggest years. His A24 film The Drama with Zendaya released in April 2026, Christopher Nolan's The Odyssey arrives July 2026, and he begins filming The Batman Part II in spring 2026. He and fiancée Suki Waterhouse also welcomed a daughter in 2024.

Did Robert Pattinson marry Suki Waterhouse?

Not officially confirmed as of April 2026 — but fans have been speculating wildly after Pattinson made a cryptic remark on The Drama press tour suggesting he and Zendaya share "the same secret." They have been engaged since 2023 and have a daughter together. Watch this space.

What is Taylor Lautner doing now in 2026?

Taylor Lautner is in development on the Amazon MGM series Taylor Lautner: Werewolf Hunter, where he plays a fictionalized version of himself as a secret supernatural tracker. He and wife Taylor Dome (both legally named Taylor Lautner since her name change) announced their first pregnancy in March 2026.

Who played Alice Cullen in Twilight?

Alice Cullen was played by Ashley Greene across all five Twilight films. Alice's gift of foresight made her one of the saga's most important supporting characters and Greene's performance made her a firm fan favourite. Greene married rugby player Paul Khoury in 2018 and has a daughter, Kingsley, born 2022.

What happened to the Cullen family actors after Twilight?

The Cullen cast have had varied but active careers: Elizabeth Reaser starred in Netflix's acclaimed Haunting of Hill House; Peter Facinelli has worked steadily in TV and film; Ashley Greene continues acting and advocacy work; Kellan Lutz starred in CBS's FBI: Most Wanted; Nikki Reed became a prominent wellness and sustainability advocate; Jackson Rathbone leads a music career alongside acting. The full Cullen cast reunited at Epic Cons Chicago in March 2024.
