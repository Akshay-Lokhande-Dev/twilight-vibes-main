Twilight Cast: Where Are They Now in 2026?

By Emma Rainsford · Updated May 2026

"From Forks to the world — the cast that defined a generation."

The last Twilight film hit theatres in 2012. That's over thirteen years ago. The three leads — Robert Pattinson, Kristen Stewart, and Taylor Lautner — were in their early twenties, barely older than the characters they played. In the years since, each of them has navigated fame in a completely different way. Here's where the entire cast stands in 2026.

The Core Three

Robert Pattinson — Edward Cullen

The brooding, century-old vampire who started it all.

🦇 Now: Hollywood A-Lister

Robert Pattinson spent the decade after Twilight systematically dismantling the heartthrob image. He chose relentlessly uncommercial projects — Cosmopolis, The Rover, Good Time, The Lighthouse — each one stranger and more demanding than the last. Critics who had dismissed him as a teen idol were forced to reckon with a genuinely remarkable actor. His performance in The Lighthouse (2019) put the debate to rest permanently.

The 2022 arrival of The Batman was Pattinson's mainstream reclamation — but on his own terms. His version of Bruce Wayne is anxious, obsessive, and nocturnal rather than heroic, which is arguably the most honest reading of the character in any adaptation. The film grossed over $770 million worldwide. A sequel has been in development, and Pattinson remains attached.

On Twilight: Pattinson has softened considerably toward the franchise in recent years. He spent much of the 2010s giving interviews that were openly ambivalent about Edward, but the Twilight Renaissance on TikTok and the genuine affection he receives from fans has shifted his tone. He now speaks about the experience with warmth, acknowledging that playing Edward gave him opportunities he would not have had otherwise. "I think I was too young to appreciate it at the time," he has said in various interviews.

Kristen Stewart — Bella Swan

The protagonist who moved to Forks and changed everything.

🎬 Now: Award-Winning Actor & Director

Kristen Stewart's post-Twilight career is one of the most complete reinventions in recent Hollywood history. She spent years working almost exclusively in European arthouse cinema — Clouds of Sils Maria (2014), Personal Shopper (2016), Lizzie (2018) — building a filmography that bore no relation to the franchise that had made her famous at seventeen.

The pivot back to prestige Hollywood came with Spencer (2021), her portrayal of Princess Diana in Pablo Larraín's impressionistic biopic. The performance earned her an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress — the first Oscar nomination for a Twilight cast member. It also completed a decade-long narrative: Kristen Stewart, who had been one of the most relentlessly mocked stars of the 2000s, was now one of the most respected actors in the industry.

Personally, Stewart came out publicly in 2017 and has been openly in a relationship with screenwriter Dylan Meyer since 2019. The couple became engaged in 2021. Her visibility as a public LGBTQ+ figure has made her a significant cultural figure to a generation of younger fans beyond the Twilight audience.

On Twilight: Stewart has been consistently more gracious about Bella than Pattinson initially was about Edward. She acknowledges the character's limitations — Bella's passivity, the criticism of the relationship — but defends the sincerity of what she brought to the role and the genuine experience of growing up on screen in front of an enormous global audience.

Taylor Lautner — Jacob Black

The Quileute wolf who offered Bella a different kind of life.

🐺 Now: Content Creator & Newlywed

Taylor Lautner's post-Twilight trajectory was the most difficult of the three leads. After the franchise ended, he struggled to find a project that capitalised on his fanbase in the way Twilight had. Several mid-budget action projects underperformed. By the mid-2010s he had largely stepped back from film work.

What happened next was unexpected: Lautner became one of the most beloved figures of the Twilight Renaissance entirely through social media. His willingness to engage with the jokes — including jokes at his own expense — made him an unlikely hero of TikTok. His self-aware appearances on his own account and as a guest on other creators' videos generated tens of millions of views. He is, paradoxically, more culturally relevant in 2026 than he was in 2015.

In 2022 he married Taylor Dome, a nurse, in a widely celebrated wedding. The couple's social media presence — often lighthearted, occasionally referencing Jacob Black directly — has been enormously popular. He has been open about the mental health challenges of navigating sudden fame at fifteen and the years of career uncertainty that followed. That honesty has deepened the affection his fanbase has for him.

On Twilight: Lautner is the most openly enthusiastic of the three leads about the franchise. He has said in multiple interviews that he is proud of the films, grateful for the fans, and that being Jacob Black remains one of the defining experiences of his life. His advice for potential new cast members — "live in the moment" — reflects genuine retrospective appreciation.

The Cullen Family

Ashley Greene — Alice Cullen

The psychic Cullen with perfect taste and boundless enthusiasm.

📺 Now: Producer & Actor

Ashley Greene has built a steady post-Twilight career across film and television. She appeared in the long-running series A Teen Thriller and has moved into producing. She married Australian actor Paul Khoury in 2018 and they have a daughter together. Greene is one of the cast members most actively involved in Twilight fan conventions and has been a consistent, enthusiastic presence at Forever Twilight in Forks events. She remains one of the most beloved supporting cast members among fans, with Alice consistently ranking as one of the franchise's most popular characters.

Kellan Lutz — Emmett Cullen

The physically powerful, playful Cullen who made every scene more fun.

🎭 Now: Actor & Entrepreneur

Kellan Lutz pursued action film work after Twilight, appearing in The Legend of Hercules (2014) and The Expendables 3 (2014). He has also moved into entrepreneurship with several brand ventures. Lutz married actress Brittany Gonzales in 2017. His relationship with the Twilight fanbase has remained warm, and he appears at fan events with evident enthusiasm.

Nikki Reed — Rosalie Hale

The most openly resentful Cullen — and, arguably, the most tragic.

🌱 Now: Activist & Author

Nikki Reed has become one of the most publicly engaged members of the Twilight cast on environmental and ethical issues. She co-founded Bayou With Love, a sustainable jewellery brand, and has been an outspoken advocate for conscious consumerism and environmental causes. She married singer-songwriter Ian Somerhalder (known for The Vampire Diaries — making the Twilight connection pleasingly circular) in 2015, and the couple have a daughter. Reed has written and spoken extensively on holistic living and her transition away from mainstream Hollywood.

Jackson Rathbone — Jasper Hale

The empath who struggled most visibly with the vegetarian lifestyle.

🎸 Now: Musician & Actor

Jackson Rathbone has continued working in both acting and music after Twilight. He performs with his band 100 Monkeys and has appeared in various film and television projects. Rathbone is one of the most unexpectedly viral Twilight cast members in the Renaissance era — Jasper's reputation as the character who always looks slightly anguished has spawned a massive meme ecosystem on TikTok. Rathbone engages with the Jasper memes with good humour. He has spoken about the challenge of playing a character whose primary expression for most of the saga is pained restraint.

Peter Facinelli — Carlisle Cullen

The patriarch — the vampire who chose compassion over centuries of conditioning.

📺 Now: Producer & Actor

Peter Facinelli continued steady television work after Twilight, most notably in the long-running series Nurse Jackie and later in The Rookie. He has also moved into producing. Facinelli divorced from co-star Jennie Garth in 2013 after twelve years of marriage and later married actress Lily Anne Harrison in 2016. He remains active on social media and engages warmly with the Twilight fanbase, attending conventions and commenting on the franchise's enduring cultural impact.

Elizabeth Reaser — Esme Cullen

The warm, architectural heart of the Cullen family.

📺 Now: Critically Acclaimed in TV

Elizabeth Reaser built one of the most impressive post-Twilight television careers in the cast. She is best known among a new generation of viewers for her role in Mike Flanagan's Netflix horror universe — The Haunting of Hill House (2018) and The Haunting of Bly Manor (2020). Her work with Flanagan positioned her as one of the finest character actors in prestige television. The Twilight connection was not lost on fans, who found some symmetry in Esme Cullen appearing in genuinely frightening supernatural horror.

The Wolf Pack

Chaske Spencer — Sam Uley

The wolf pack's alpha, carrying the weight of the first transformation.

🏔️ Now: Acclaimed in Indigenous Cinema

Chaske Spencer has become one of the most recognised Indigenous actors in Hollywood, with significant roles in major productions including the Paramount+ series Yellowstone prequel 1923. His casting in high-profile projects has coincided with a broader industry reckoning around authentic Indigenous representation. Spencer is a member of the Fort Peck Assiniboine and Sioux Tribes and has spoken candidly about what it meant to play a Quileute character as a Native American actor, and the responsibilities that came with that.

The Forks Humans

Anna Kendrick — Jessica Stanley

Bella's enthusiastic, slightly overwhelming best friend at Forks High.

🎵 Now: A-List Star

Anna Kendrick's post-Twilight career trajectory is among the most successful in the cast. She received an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actress for Up in the Air (2009) while still filming the Twilight sequels. The Pitch Perfect franchise (2012, 2015, 2017) made her a bankable lead. She has continued working across film and television and authored a memoir, Scrappy Little Nobody (2016), which was a bestseller. Kendrick has been openly, hilariously nostalgic about Twilight in interviews and on social media, leaning into the franchise with genuine warmth.

Billy Burke — Charlie Swan

Bella's quietly awkward, deeply devoted father. The most relatable human in the saga.

📺 Now: Steady Character Actor

Billy Burke continued building one of the most respected character acting careers in the franchise's supporting cast. He starred in the NBC series Revolution (2012–2014) and has appeared in numerous film and television projects. Burke is frequently cited in discussions of the best performances in the Twilight films — his portrayal of Charlie is grounded, real, and quietly funny in ways that anchor the more heightened emotional reality around him.

The Original Villains

Cam Gigandet — James

The tracker. The first and most viscerally frightening villain of the saga.

🎬 Now: Working Actor

Cam Gigandet has continued working in film and television since Twilight, appearing in a range of projects including Free Style and various television roles. He remains one of the more underrated parts of the original Twilight film — James is genuinely menacing in a way the later Volturi villains never quite match, and Gigandet plays him with a chilling economy that the role deserved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Did Robert Pattinson and Kristen Stewart actually date? Yes. Pattinson and Stewart were in a relationship from approximately 2009 to 2013, overlapping with the Twilight filming period. Their relationship ended publicly in 2013. Both have since moved on — Pattinson married singer FKA twigs (though they later separated) and is now in a relationship with Suki Waterhouse; Stewart became engaged to Dylan Meyer. The relationship was intensely covered by tabloid media at the time and remains one of the most discussed aspects of the franchise's cultural footprint.

Is Taylor Lautner still acting? Lautner stepped back significantly from acting in the mid-2010s after a run of projects that underperformed commercially. He has focused more on social media and personal life, though he has expressed openness to the right acting opportunity. His marriage and content creation work have made him one of the most visible former Twilight cast members on social platforms.

Who from the Twilight cast has won awards? Kristen Stewart received an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress for Spencer (2021) — the first Oscar nomination for a principal Twilight cast member. Anna Kendrick received an Oscar nomination for Best Supporting Actress for Up in the Air (2009) while still filming the Twilight sequels, making her the first from the cast to receive that recognition.

Are any original cast members returning for the Netflix animated series? No casting has been confirmed for the Midnight Sun animated series on Netflix as of 2026. Robert Pattinson has joked about returning but nothing is official. Given that it is an animated series, all voice roles will be new castings.

Which cast members attend Twilight fan conventions? Ashley Greene, Kellan Lutz, Jackson Rathbone, Peter Facinelli, and Billy Burke have all appeared at Twilight fan conventions and the annual Forever Twilight in Forks festival in Washington State. The three principal leads (Pattinson, Stewart, Lautner) have not attended dedicated Twilight events in recent years, though all three interact positively with the franchise on social media.
