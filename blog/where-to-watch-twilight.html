Beyond the Films: The Extended Universe

If you've finished the marathon and want more, the Twilight universe extends beyond the five films. "Midnight Sun" (2020) retells the original novel from Edward's perspective and is available as a standalone book, not a film. A Netflix animated series adaptation has also been in development, expanding the story beyond what the live-action films covered — check our Midnight Sun Netflix guide for the latest status.

Best way to watch as a group: Use a synced watch-party feature (Max, Amazon Prime Video, and Teleparty for browser-based viewing all support this) so friends in different locations can react together in real time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Twilight on Netflix in 2026?

In the USA, the Twilight films moved from Netflix to HBO Max (Max) on January 1, 2026. In some other countries, Netflix may still hold streaming rights — check your local Netflix library.

Is Twilight free anywhere?

The Twilight films are included with an active Max subscription in the USA. There is no fully free, legal streaming option without a subscription or rental fee. Be cautious of unofficial sites offering "free" streams as these violate copyright law.

Can I download Twilight to watch offline?

Yes — Max allows downloads for offline viewing for subscribers. Amazon Prime Video, Apple TV, and Vudu also allow offline downloads for purchased or rented films depending on the plan.

What order should I watch the Twilight movies?

Watch in release order: Twilight (2008) → New Moon (2009) → Eclipse (2010) → Breaking Dawn Part 1 (2011) → Breaking Dawn Part 2 (2012). Then optionally read Midnight Sun and wait for the Netflix animated series.
