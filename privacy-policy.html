Overview

TwilightVibes.com ("we", "our", "the site") is an independent fan travel guide to the real-world locations of the Twilight Saga, operated by Emma Rainsford. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymised data including pages visited, time on site, approximate geographic location (country/region level), device type, and referring website. This data does not identify you personally. Google Analytics data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Contact communications. If you email us at twilightvibespnw@gmail.com, we retain your message for the purpose of responding to it. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

We do not collect names, addresses, payment information, or any personally identifying information through the site itself. There are no account registrations, no comment systems, and no forms that collect personal data beyond voluntary email contact.

Cookies

Our site uses the following cookies:

Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat) — used to distinguish users and sessions for analytics purposes. These are persistent cookies set by Google.

Google AdSense cookies — used to serve relevant advertisements. See the section below for more detail.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using any content on TwilightVibes, but may affect the personalisation of advertisements you see.

Google AdSense & Advertising

TwilightVibes uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites. For more information, see How Google uses data when you use partners' sites.

Third-Party Links

Our content includes links to third-party websites (booking platforms, streaming services, merchandise retailers). Once you click a link and leave TwilightVibes, this privacy policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Children's Privacy

TwilightVibes is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy regulations, including the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal personal data (only voluntary email communications), most requests can be addressed by simply emailing us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
