By using TwilightVibes.com, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily. If you contact us via email at twilightvibespnw@gmail.com, we collect your email address and the content of your message solely for the purpose of responding to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit opt-in consent.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, TwilightVibes automatically receives and records certain information from your browser when you visit our Site. This is standard practice across the internet and does not identify you personally.

Log Files

TwilightVibes follows standard industry practice in using log files. These files record visitor activity when users visit websites. The information collected by log files includes Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring and exit pages, and the number of clicks. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information. The purpose is to analyse trends, administer the site, track users' movement around the site, and gather demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

TwilightVibes uses cookies — small text files placed on your device — to enhance your experience and enable our advertising partners to serve relevant ads. Like any other website, we use cookies. These cookies store information including visitors' preferences and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google Analytics Cookies (_ga, _gid, _gat_gtag): Used to collect anonymised data about how visitors interact with our site, including pages viewed, time on site, referral source, device type, and approximate geographic region. This data does not identify you personally. Google Analytics data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Web Beacons. Some pages on our site and some of our email communications may contain electronic images known as web beacons (also called single-pixel gifs). Web beacons allow us and our third-party advertising partners to count the number of users who have visited specific pages, delivered certain emails, and to help determine the effectiveness of our promotional campaigns.

Google AdSense and the DoubleClick DART Cookie

TwilightVibes uses Google AdSense to display third-party advertisements on our site. Google, as a third-party advertising vendor, uses cookies to serve ads to our visitors based on their visit to TwilightVibes.com and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. Visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

We have implemented the following Google advertising features: Remarketing with Google AdSense; Google Display Network Impression Reporting; DoubleClick Platform Integration; and Demographics and Interests Reporting.

Along with vendors such as Google, we use first-party cookies (such as the Google Analytics cookie) and third-party cookies (such as the DoubleClick cookie) together to inform, optimise, and serve ads based on someone's past visits to our website. You can set preferences for how Google advertises to you using the Google Ad Preferences page, and if you want, you can opt out of interest-based advertising entirely by visiting aboutads.info.

CCPA Privacy Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:

Right to Know. You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell about you.

Right to Delete. You have the right to request that we delete personal information we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.

Right to Opt-Out. You have the right to opt out of the sale of your personal information. TwilightVibes does not sell personal information. We do share data with Google for analytics and advertising purposes as described above.

Right to Non-Discrimination. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at: twilightvibespnw@gmail.com. We will respond to verifiable consumer requests within 45 days.

GDPR Rights (EEA and UK Residents)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR:

Right of Access. You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification. You have the right to request that we correct inaccurate personal data.

Right to Erasure. You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, subject to applicable law.

Right to Restrict Processing. You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

Right to Data Portability. You have the right to receive personal data you have provided to us in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object. You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing. Where we process personal data, we do so on the basis of: (a) your consent; (b) our legitimate interests in operating and improving this website; or (c) compliance with legal obligations. Given the minimal nature of data we collect (primarily anonymised analytics), most processing is based on legitimate interests.

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at twilightvibespnw@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
