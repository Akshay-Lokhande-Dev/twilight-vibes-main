About TwilightVibes

TwilightVibes is the definitive fan-travel guide to the world of the Twilight Saga — covering real filming locations in Forks, Washington and St. Helens, Oregon; character deep-dives; the Twilight Core aesthetic; and everything fans need to plan their Pacific Northwest pilgrimage.

The site covers: Forks, Washington · La Push Beach · St. Helens, Oregon · Portland filming locations · Vancouver, BC · Character guides (Edward, Bella, Jacob, the Volturi) · Twilight Core aesthetic · The upcoming Netflix animated series.
